<p>Anjukan Kathirgamanathan is a Senior Quant and Machine Learning Engineer at Sympower helping build software to enable the transition to a smart and renewable power grid globally. Anjukan has completed a Post Graduate Diploma in Business and Management at Queen's University Belfast and in addition a PhD at the Energy Institute of University College Dublin. His research looked at the use of data-driven predictive control in buildings, thereby harnessing energy flexibility to allow increasing renewable energy penetration in the future smart electricity grid.</p>

<p>Anjukan completed his BE(Hons) at the University of Auckland, New Zealand specialising in Mechanical Engineering. He is a visiting lecturer at the University of Jaffna teaching sustainability for engineers, is a former WEF Global Shaper, a leadership network member of the Asia New Zealand Foundation and a Rotary Young Leaders 
Award recipient. In his spare time, he loves to play touch rugby, read Terry Pratchett or social pyschology books or spend time doing anything active outdoors</p>
